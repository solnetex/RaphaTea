Catering & Events

From weddings and corporate meetings to birthdays and school fundraisers — we bring the joy of fresh, organic tea and boba to you.

Packages

Pop‑Up Boba Bar (On‑Site)
We set up a live bar with staff to shake, pour, and serve. Includes cups, straws, lids, ice, toppings, and a curated menu of 3–5 drinks.
Pre‑Batched Dispensers
Large insulated dispensers of your favorite teas with toppings and cups on the side. Perfect for self‑serve stations.
Bulk Cup Pickup
Order sealed drinks in bulk for quick pickup at our shop. Ideal for office treats and classroom celebrations.

How It Works

1) Inquiry

Tell us your date, guest count, location, and preferences.

2) Proposal

We’ll recommend a package, menu, and quote tailored to your event.

3) Confirmation

Secure your date with a deposit. We finalize logistics and menu.

4) Event Day

We arrive early to set up and keep drinks flowing on schedule.

What’s Included

All Essentials
